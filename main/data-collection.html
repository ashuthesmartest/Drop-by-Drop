          Data Collection

          We did extensive Data Collection in the Delhi NCR region. We prepared survey sheets, opinion polls for the purpose of data collection. We went to people and discussed various problems related to water and made them realize the importance of water.    The main survey areas were: Alpha-1, Beta-2, Gamma-2, Psi-6, Swarnagri, Alpha-2, Sigma-1, Sector-37, Sector-8, Janakpuri, DLF, etc. and a number of educational institutions.
          We went through various government websites, journals, books, newspapers etc.

          We also conducted interviews with high officials of numerous organizations and agencies.
          1.    Sh. B.C Ghosh, CEO, NTPC HYDRO
          2.    Sh. S.N Rawat, SDO, Irrigation Department, Bulandshahr
          3.    Scientist Sarvesh Rai and R.K Ojha, PTL, Greater NOIDA
