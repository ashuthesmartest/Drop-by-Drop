          Impact on Community

          Our success of awareness campaign and our site is as follows:

          Impact in our school
          In our school, we noted the number of times the water coolers was refilled, amount of water expelled and leaking taps. We took help from the site http://tinyurl.com/yjkgscn and tried to fulfill its objectives. After the campaign, following results were observed:

          1.    Number of refilling of water coolers went down by 10 to 8 times per day in two weeks. This means, now there is less wastage of water and students are making proper use of it.

          2.    Amount of water expelled was nearly 70000 litres per day. After the campaigning, water expelled went down by 30% i.e. 49000 litres per day. Dish-washing methods and sanitation area was made water-efficient.

          3.    After our investigation in our school, the leaking and faulty taps found went down from 16 to 5 in just two weeks. It means school is now taking a proper action against leaking taps.

          Impact in our region
          In Greater NOIDA, we went to many developing apartments. We told them about our research and recommendations. Managers approved our recommendations and left as much soft area as possible. Societies like Golf Vista, Eldeco and Jal Vayu Vihar followed our recommendations.
